Belly-Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

This dashboards shows information about volunteers who participated in a belly button survery. It includes the demographic information, and visualizations around the belly button bacterial data for each volunteer, the top 10 bacterial species in a volunteers belly button and a speedometer like visualization of each volunteer's belly button wash frequency. The visualization helps volunteers identify whether a species that Improbable Beef identifies as a candidate to manufacture synthetic beef, is found in their navel.
